# Davis's Big Beautiful Workout App Support

If you need help with Davis's Big Beautiful Workout App, use the contact details below.

Contact:

- Email: davis.deaton@verizon.net

## Common topics

- Health permissions not enabled
- iCloud backup or import/export not available
- CSV import issues
- Gym search or gym tagging questions
- Workout history or insight discrepancies

## Before you contact support

- Confirm the app is updated to the latest version.
- Confirm Apple Health permissions are enabled if you expect Health-based insights.
- Confirm iCloud Drive is enabled on your device if you expect iCloud document storage.
- Include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
